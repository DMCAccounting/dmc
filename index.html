How We Work

Our Simple 4-Step Process

Getting started is easy — whether you're in Colombo, Kandy, Jaffna, or anywhere in Sri Lanka.

1

Free Initial Consultation

We begin with a free, no-obligation consultation — by phone, video call, WhatsApp, or in person at our Ampara office — to understand your business needs and goals.

2

Document Collection & Review

We collect your financial documents, bank statements, invoices, and records — in person or submitted digitally. Our team reviews everything thoroughly before proceeding.

Professional Processing & Filing

Our expert accountants process your accounts, prepare financial statements, calculate tax liabilities, and file all required returns with the IRD and other authorities on time.

Reporting & Year-Round Support
